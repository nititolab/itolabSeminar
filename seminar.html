出欠席を確認するため、出席・欠席どちらの場合もリンク先のフォームから参加意志を報告してください。通常、ゼミ開催日の2週間前までに報告してください。

Please confirm your attendance for the seminar with a linked form. Usually, let's report it two weeks before the seminar opening day.
The seminar is to attend in principle. When you are absent from the seminar, you must describe a reason on the form.
Only Professor Takayuki Ito and some administrators of the form can get your information entered in it. In addition, the information is not used other than seminar attendance confirmation.
